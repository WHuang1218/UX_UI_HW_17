ABOUT

Hi! Let's get to know each other.
Here is some information about me.

My name is Jason Huang and I’m a UX/UI designer in Portland, Oregon.


My journey into UX/UI started when I made the decision to change career away from medicine. With a lot of introspection, I found a common thread in wanting to understand and help people, but this time through design. I’m always looking to improve myself by continuing to learn and develop my skills everyday.

In my free time, I enjoy playing basketball, building plastic model kits, and flying my drone to take beautiful sceneries of places that I visited.
